Cleanliness drive

The children we teach maintain a little cleanliness and hygiene. Cleanliness drive teaching children basic sweeping, washing hands and using bins etc. The children showed great enthusiasm in learning all these things and implementing them in their day to day life. Hope they implement all the good things in their life.

Cycle Distribution

It is really important for one to be physically fit for working efficiently. Thus to realize this is the group had collected some discarded bicycles from the institute and distributed them to the children. this is not only helped a child in recreation but also helped to commute from place to place faster. Once again, Thank you, donors!!!

School Admission

We witnessed many children not admitted to the school. It was due to low income of the family or lack of awareness of parents. Thus we made a decision to admit each and every child to school. So far many children have been admitted to the school. Many are remaining but we expect them to get admitted very soon.

Book Fair

The children, knowing little about the literature were brought to the book fair which helped them in broadening their thoughts and views. A child was gifted a few books to help her know more about life and how to succeed in life. Once again the donors helped a lot to make the child stride from darkness to light.

Study Material Distribution

The low income of children is also a barrier to accessing good education. Even a cheaper book is unaffordable for them. Thus we distributed many second-hand reference books and textbooks to children which were collected from students from outside and from the institute. The book donors did a splendid job by helping children strive towards enlightenment.

Slipper Distribution

Walking barefoot is extremely hot weather is really a painful task. Thus it was decided to relieve innocent children from this pain and gift them a pair of slippers. Funds were raised for the Slippers distribution derive and many pairs were brought from it. It is once again a kind gesture of all donors that now children don't walk barefoot.

Cloth Distribution

Blocking, a basic necessity wasn't getting fulfilled for the children living in slums. We thus organised a cloth distribution drive where we collected the discarded clothes from the students and staff at the institute and distributed among the needy. We also organised a winter cloth distribution drive where we had brought winter clothes from the funds raised for the same. The children of Nilam society are really very grateful to all donors.

Awarness Program

We had also conducted an awareness program for kids and parents informing them bout career opportunities in future. The presentation also included some cleanliness time management and result in analysis tips. Apart from this we also gave some guidelines on effective parenting. The parents were really grateful for such an uprising presentation.
