History

The founder of the group. Prince Verma had been enlightening the necessitous children for two years before the establishment of the group. It was on 16 June 2019 that the official group with the name 'Social India' was started. Since then it has been progressing swiftly and is now enlightening/enlightened over 60 children. It came up with its tagline 'HOPE FOR OTHER' in December 2019. It was January 2020 when it changed to Edignite Educational and charitable trust and had applied for registering itself as an NGO. Apart from the needy, the group has also thought about the welfare of students of the institute and has come up with many applications, Youtube videos. Notes..for the same. The group now has 20 to 25 volunteers and is still expanding.

About Children

We are teaching the needy children since long. They are really adorable and innocent, unaware of the outside world. They often welcome us delightfully whenever we go to teach them. often we are offered hand made cards/gifts and also tasty meals cooked at their homes.apart from this they are so connected to us that they want to spend a maximum of their time in gaining knowledge from us. We hope that we may be remembered forever by children in case we depart.
