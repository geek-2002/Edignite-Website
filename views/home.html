Message from founder

Social India group always works for the needy of the nation. The group aims at providing all help to the necessitous people of the nation. Apart from education, the group tries to provide spread smiles amongst children by celebrating many festivals, playing, making them participate in several events and thus spending a good amount of time with them. Thus, the group also aims at building a strong relationship with the children which can, in the future lead to their betterment. The group volunteers are on toes to help the underprivileged children and believe service to them as their duty towards the nation and thus the globe. This virtue of selflessness is the thing I like the most about the group. Working with volunteers for the betterment of the children and interacting with several needy ones adds to experiences and memories that can never be erased from my life, I hope all the readers to do some work that benefits the society, nation and the world, may it be small help or toughest job of eradicating poverty. My best wishes are always there with everyone.

About Us

The founder of the group. Prince Verma had been enlightening the necessitous children for two years before the establishment of the group. It was on 16 June 2019 that the official group with the name 'Social India' was started.Since then it has been progressing swiftly and is now enlightening/enlightened over 60 children. It came up with its tagline 'HOPE FOR OTHER' in December 2019.

It was January 2020 when it changed to Edignite Educational and charitable trust and had applied for registering itself as an NGO. Apart from the needy, the group has also thought about the welfare of students of the institute and has come up with many applications, Youtube videos. Notes..for the same. The group now has 20 to 25 volunteers and is still expanding.

About Children

We are teaching the needy children since long. They are really adorable and innocent, unaware of the outside world. They often welcome us delightfully whenever we go to teach them. often we are offered hand made cards/gifts and also tasty meals cooked at their homes.apart from this they are so connected to us that they want to spend a maximum of their time in gaining knowledge from us. We hope that we may be remembered forever by children in case we depart.

Reliable services

There are many places where we go to teach children. The places they live in are pitiful,We provide help to children by giving them resources to study,We make children participate in dancing performances, drawing, competitions, workshops etc

Great support

Many children are also incapable of meeting their nutritional needs. Thus to fill up their stomachs, we regularly pack some food from our hostel mess and distribute it among children. We feel really happy when we witness the children's empty stomachs getting filled. many blissful moments are captured and many relationships with children are built during this time.

Food Distribution

Many children are also incapable of meeting their nutritional needs. Thus to fill up their stomachs, we regularly pack some food from our hostel mess and distribute it among children. We feel really happy when we witness the children's empty stomachs getting filled. many blissful moments are captured and many relationships with children are built during this time.

Making Children Par with Others

Apart from the prescribed syllabus, we prepare children for several competitive examinations such as NTSE, UPSC, GPSC, Navodaya. We also strive for giving them a National level platform where they can showcase their academic strength We make children participate in dancing performances, drawing, competitions, workshops etc.organised by our institute. This also provides the platform and an opportunity to show their talents. Several reviews and appreciation from the audience and encourage them to keep going.

Celebrating With Children

We also celebrate our birthdays with children and offer them notebooks stationary food packets etc.Also we are gifted with hand made birthday cards from the children The children are much eager to spend blissful moments with us. We thus try celebrating many festivals with them. This is the best time when our bonding with the children strengthens. We really feel elated when we spend a festival time with them
